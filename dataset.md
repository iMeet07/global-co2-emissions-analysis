Dataset Description

Dataset Title: CO₂ and Greenhouse Gas Emissions Dataset
Source: Our World in Data
Dataset Link: https://github.com/owid/co2-data

Brief Description of the Dataset
This dataset contains global information on carbon dioxide (CO₂) and other greenhouse gas emissions across countries and years. It includes environmental, economic, and energy-related indicators such as total CO₂ emissions, emissions per capita, methane emissions, energy consumption, GDP, and population.

The dataset contains over 40,000 observations and more than 70 variables, including both continuous variables (e.g., CO₂ emissions, GDP, population, energy consumption) and categorical variables (e.g., country). Because data availability varies across countries and years, the dataset contains missing values, making it suitable for demonstrating data preprocessing and cleaning techniques.

Relevance to the Research Questions
This dataset allows us to explore relationships between economic development, energy consumption, and greenhouse gas emissions. The project will analyze how different economic and environmental factors influence CO₂ emissions across countries.

The dataset is appropriate for applying statistical methods such as regression modeling, clustering, and principal component analysis (PCA) to understand global emission patterns and identify key factors associated with climate change.

Dataset Characteristics
Number of observations (n): 40,000+
Number of variables (p): 70+
Continuous variables: CO₂ emissions, GDP, energy consumption, population, methane emissions
Categorical variables: Country
Data domain: Environmental and economic data
